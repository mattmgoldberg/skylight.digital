Here are some of the tools and resources that we use at Skylight to help us and our clients deliver better public services. These toolkits are always being iterated on and are available to everyone under our public-domain license. Feel free to copy and adapt them as you choose.

Digital Talent Management Handbook

A comprehensive guide on how government agencies can recruit, hire, onboard, and retain digital talent.

Data Sharing Playbook

A guide for state & local agencies on how to improve cross-organizational data sharing.

Accessibility Guide

Tools and practices making products and services accessible to everyone.

Agile Procurement Playbook

A guide on how to apply the principles of agile to procurement.

These toolkits are managed by our Communities of Practice. Communities are organized groups within Skylight who are passionate about developing and sharing our expertise with the world. We currently have nine communities of practice. These include:

  • Delivery Management, led by Victor Zapanta (victor@skylight.digital)
  • Research & Design, led by Victor Zapanta (victor@skylight.digital)
  • Product, led by Victor Zapanta (victor@skylight.digital)
  • Engineering, led by Victor Zapanta (victor@skylight.digital)
  • Data, led by Victor Zapanta (victor@skylight.digital)
  • Procurement, led by Chris Cairns (chris@skylight.digital)
  • Transformation, led by Victor Zapanta (victor@skylight.digital)
  • Accessibility, led by Nick Bristow (nick@skylight.digital)
  • Internet of Things, led by Geoff Mulligan (geoff@skylight.digital)
